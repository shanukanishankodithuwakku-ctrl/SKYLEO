THE SKYLEO ORIGIN

📊 Nations Lanka Finance PLC

Learning finance from the ground up. Manual statements, journal entries, deep understanding of how companies actually work — taxes, income, expenses, assets, liabilities. This is where discipline was built.

🌍 WNS Global Services

From domestic to global. Working with international clients, learning how BPOs operate, mastering cost structures, billing frameworks, and the reality of what clients actually need versus what they're sold.

🏢 EY GDS — Finance Consultant

Mastering enterprise systems. ERP modelling, Power BI dashboards, SAP S/4HANA, client management, resource allocation. Delivering enterprise solutions — at enterprise prices.
